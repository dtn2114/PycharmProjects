<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>Item 4.&#9;Purpose of Transaction</B></P>

Jinan, Shandong 250101 PRC. At the Special Meeting, the Issuer&rsquo;s stockholders voted in favor to approve the previously disclosed
Agreement and Plan of Merger, dated May 16, 2012 (the &ldquo;Merger Agreement&rdquo;), by and among the Issuer, Parent and Genius
Choice Capital Limited (&ldquo;Merger Sub&rdquo;), providing for the merger of Merger Sub with and into the Issuer (the &ldquo;Merger&rdquo;),
with the Issuer surviving the merger as a wholly-owned subsidiary of Parent.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">On November&nbsp;9, 2012, the Issuer filed the Articles of Merger
with the Corporate Registry of British Virgin Islands, pursuant to which the Merger became effective. Upon consummation of the
Merger, each outstanding Ordinary Share (other than as described below) was converted automatically into the right to receive $4.15
in cash (the &ldquo;Per Share Merger Consideration&rdquo;), without interest and less any applicable withholding taxes.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Shares of Common Stock owned by the Reporting Persons were cancelled
without payment of the Per Share Merger Consideration. No shareholder has made a demand for appraisal rights under the BVI Business
Companies Act, 2004, as amended.&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Upon the effective time of the Merger, the Issuer became a wholly-owned
subsidiary of Parent with 50,000 shares of Common Stock outstanding (all of which became owned by Parent) and the separate corporate
existence of Merger Sub ceased. As a result of the Merger, the Common Stock ceased to trade on The NASDAQ Capital Market&nbsp;
(&ldquo;NASDAQ&rdquo;) prior to the opening of trading on November 12, 2012 and the Issuer became eligible for delisting from NASDAQ
and termination of registration pursuant to Rules&nbsp;12g-4(a)(1)&nbsp;and 12h-3(b)(1)(i)&nbsp;of the Exchange Act. NASDAQ has
filed an application on Form&nbsp;25 with the SEC to report that the Issuer is no longer listed on NASDAQ. On November 19, 2012,
the Issuer will file a Certification and Notice of Termination on Form15 with the SEC in order to deregister the Common Stock under
the Exchange Act and suspend the Issuer&rsquo;s reporting obligations under the Exchange Act.</P>
